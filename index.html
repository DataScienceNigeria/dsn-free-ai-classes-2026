About the Platform

Digital Skills Nigeria is a Microsoft-led initiative supporting the Nigerian government in empowering youth (ages 16–35) with digital skills. The platform offers free access to AI, cloud computing, and data science resources — all powered by Microsoft technology.

You will be enrolled on the Microsoft AI Fluency program — a beginner-friendly path covering six key areas of modern AI.

AI Basics Generative AI AI Ethics Microsoft Copilot AI in Search AI's Social Impact

How to Get Started

  1. 1Visit digitalskillsnigeria.org
  2. 2Click "Register" or "Get Started"
  3. 3Create your account using your registered email
  4. 4Log in and navigate to the "AI Fluency" learning path
  5. 5Enrol and start learning immediately

Track 02

Data Storytelling → Microsoft Learn (PL-300)

learn.microsoft.com ↗

About the Platform

Microsoft Learn is Microsoft's official free learning platform. You will be enrolled on the Data Analyst Associate (PL-300) certification learning path, which prepares you for the globally recognised Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst certification.

Before You Begin — You Will Need:

  • ✅ A free Microsoft Account — sign up at signup.live.com
  • ✅ Power BI Desktop — download free from powerbi.microsoft.com
  • ✅ Basic understanding of data (helpful, not required)
  • ✅ 7 days of dedicated learning time
Power BI Data Modeling Power Query DAX Dashboards Report Design

How to Get Started

  1. 1Visit learn.microsoft.com
  2. 2Click "Sign In" at the top right
  3. 3Create a free Microsoft Account if you don't have one
  4. 4Search for "Data Analyst Associate"
  5. 5Select the PL-300 learning path
  6. 6Click "Start" and begin learning!
💡 Download Power BI Desktop before starting — many hands-on labs require it.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my track after registration?
Track changes are generally not permitted once a cohort begins. Please choose carefully during registration. If you have an urgent reason, contact DSN support as early as possible.
What if I miss the 7-day deadline?
Participants who do not complete their track within 7 days will not receive a certificate for that cohort. You may re-register for the next weekly cohort within May 2026.
Do I need prior experience to join?
No! All 4 tracks are designed for complete beginners. No prior knowledge of AI, coding, or data science is required to get started.
When does each cohort start?
Cohorts start every week throughout May 2026. You will be notified of your cohort start date via email after registration. Each cohort lasts exactly one week (7 days).
What is a Microsoft Exam Voucher?
Microsoft exam vouchers allow you to write an official Microsoft certification exam (e.g., PL-300 for Power BI, AZ-900 for Azure Fundamentals) completely free. These are distributed to the top performers across all tracks and cohorts.
How do I know which platform to use?
Your onboarding email will specify your exact platform and provide login instructions based on your chosen track. The platform guides on this page also summarise everything you need.
Is the training really free?
Yes — 100% free. DSN Free AI Classes has been offering completely free AI training to beginners since 2019. There are no hidden charges, subscription fees, or costs of any kind.
Can I participate from outside Nigeria?
Yes! While the programme is primarily targeted at Nigerians, participants from across Africa and beyond are welcome to join. All platforms and resources are accessible online from anywhere.
